Insight

Rails has evolved from a creator-centric kingdom into a multi-architect civilization.

David Heinemeier Hansson created the gravitational center — Design 100, Indispensability 100, Gravity 100. But unlike Laravel or esbuild, multiple architects now share structural authority. Jeremy Kemper (Design 89), Rafael Franca (68.4), Aaron Patterson (54), Xavier Noria (45.8), and Jose Valim (39.4) — six people with Design above 35.

This is extraordinary for a framework-driven project. Top10 Design of 51.2 exceeds most Go projects. Rails is "framework-driven" in its user-facing API, but internally it functions more like a self-structured project with distributed governance.

Gravity Concentration of 0.9% is among the lowest measured — comparable to Kubernetes (0.8%) and Rust compiler (0.6%). Rails has achieved architect succession at scale.
